Our Story

Welcome to Matodi todi, where we are committed to nurturing the earth, empowering communities, and cultivating a sustainable future.

Founded by passionate women in South Africa, Matodi todi is rooted in the belief that agriculture is not only about growing food but about growing opportunities for all.

We wanted to create a business that not only thrived but also made a positive impact on the environment and society. Our journey began in 2015 with a vision: to provide quality gardening services, fresh local pork, and innovative urban farming solutions

At the heart of our mission is our commitment to using sustainable farming practices. From implementing water-efficient techniques to promoting biodiversity and reducing food waste, we are constantly seeking ways to protect our planet while providing fresh, nutritious produce to our customers.

We take pride in sourcing much of our produce from marginalized communities where we partner to provide training, fair wages, and access to markets. By empowering individuals in these communities, we are able to not only fight food insecurity but also uplift lives, create jobs, and strengthen local economies.

Our urban farming services bring the joys of gardening to city dwellers, offering solutions that allow them to grow their own food in small spaces, from balconies to backyards. We believe that everyone, no matter where they live, should have the ability to cultivate their own fresh, healthy food.

We also offer premium, ethically raised pork, sourced from farms that prioritize animal welfare and environmental stewardship. Our animals are raised with care, in accordance with the highest standards, ensuring that our products are not only delicious but also responsibly produced.

As a woman-led business, we are deeply passionate about creating opportunities for women in agriculture. We believe in breaking down barriers, supporting women in leadership roles, and championing equality across all sectors.

At Matodi todi, we are more than just a business; we are a movement. A movement toward a more sustainable, equitable, and empowered future for our communities and our planet.
